About Us

Welcome! We are Gael Force Robotics Team 5327A from Dublin High School. Our team is made up of divers and passionate individuals who aim to win the world championship. Team 5327A has already qualified to the world championships multiple times in it's past, and has even been in it's division semifinals at World's. We firmly believe that with all of this experience, paired with our excellent team dynamic, we can achieve this goal.
